Red Pepper Chicken
Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
15 minutes 6 hours 6 hours 15 minutes
Ingredients Instructions
  • 2lbs chicken breast
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • One small onion
  • One medium red bell pepper
  1. Cut up red bell pepper and onion
  2. Mince garlic
  3. Combine all ingredients and place in crockpot
  4. Cook on low for 6 hours or until chicken can easily be shredded
Stuffed Peppers
Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
25 minutes 4 hours 4 hours 15 minutes
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1lb ground beef
  • One small onion
  • One garlic clove, minced
  • 4 small bell peppers, tops cut off
  • 24oz pasta sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  1. Coat bottom of slow cooker with olive oil
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and 2 tablespoons of pasta sauce
  3. Stuff peppers evenly with mixture
  4. Place peppers in slow cooker and top with remanining sauce and water
  5. Cook on low for 6 hours or until meat is cooked through
